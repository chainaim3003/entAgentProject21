LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2026, between
APEX TEXTILE IMPORTS INC., a Delaware corporation ("Borrower"), and FIRST
NATIONAL BANK OF NEW YORK, a national banking association ("Lender").

1. PRINCIPAL AMOUNT
The Lender hereby agrees to extend a term loan facility to the Borrower in the
principal amount of One Million United States Dollars (USD 1,000,000) under
the terms set forth herein.

2. INTEREST RATE
The Loan shall bear interest at the Floating Rate, defined as the Secured
Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus a credit spread of 250 basis points
(2.50%) per annum, calculated and reset on a monthly basis. Interest shall be
payable in arrears on the last business day of each calendar month.

3. TERM
The Loan shall have a maturity of twenty-four (24) months from the Origination
Date (January 15, 2026), with a stated Maturity Date of January 15, 2028.

4. CURRENCY
All disbursements, principal repayments, accrued interest, and fees shall be
denominated and payable in United States Dollars (USD).

5. USE OF PROCEEDS
The Borrower confirms that the Loan proceeds shall be used exclusively for the
financing of woven textile fabric imports (HS commodity code 5208, GTAP
classification "tex") sourced from suppliers located in the Republic of India,
destined for wholesale and retail distribution in the United States.

6. REPAYMENT
Principal shall be repaid in full on the Maturity Date (bullet repayment).
Interest shall be paid monthly as set forth in Section 2.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
of the State of New York.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date
first written above.
